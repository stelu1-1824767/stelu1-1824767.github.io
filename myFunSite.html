All things Choco Taco

Enjoy vanilla with fudgy swirls wrapped in a delicious, sugar-cone taco — then drenched in a thick, chocolatey coating and sprinkled with peanuts. Ice cream fans and taco lovers can finally come together with this tasty dessert taco. And the best part is that there’s no hot sauce required on one of these creamy, delicious desserts! Though, if you want to throw some chili peppers on it, we won’t judge. Take one bite and you’ll discover why Choco Taco is the one dessert that both ice cream trucks and taco trucks universally agree on!
